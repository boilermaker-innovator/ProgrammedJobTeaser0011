About the company: Programmed employs over 30,000 people every year across a broad range of industries and partners with more than 10,000 customers, small and large.

About the role:

We are currently seeking Boilermakers in Kalgoorlie. Local Residents encouraged to apply. FIFO from Perth Considered. Working Monday to Friday | 9 Hour Days | This role is commencing ASAP.

Successful applicants will have previous mine site or refinery experience, with a strong work ethic and demonstrated commitment to safety.

Skills and Experience:

  • Australian Trade Certificate
  • Pipe Welding Experience (highly regarded)
  • Nationally Accredited Confined Space
  • Nationally Accredited Working at Heights
  • Construction White Card
  • High risk licenses (highly regarded)
  • Previous mining and/or shutdown experience (highly regarded)
  • Demonstrated commitment to safety
  • Ability to pass a pre-employment drug and alcohol screen
